About Zunder Zifflin


Zunder Zifflin is an international company based in Alexandra, Virginia. Being formed in November 2002 by Henry Steele & Andrew Copperfield. Zunder Zifflin starts just as a paper producing company back then, but because of the increasing demands in Office Supplies, Printers, and many things related to stationeries and etc. So, we also decided to create many kinds of products that can help our customers to increase their productivity and get their job done. Zunder Zifflin provides its customers quality office and information technology products, furniture, printing values and the expertise required for making informed buying choices. We provide our products and services with a dedication to the highest degree of integrity and quality of customer satisfaction, developing long-term professional relationships with employees that develop pride, creating a stable working environment and company spirit.By today, we are previleged to distribute our products through 68 countries, including those in Europe and Asia, connecting us with a millions of customers everyday with our exceptional and high quality products.
